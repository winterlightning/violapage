Viola is your social media radio

Instead of constantly checking your facebook account for updates, Viola reads your newsfeed out loud as background audio.

Get from Chrome Store (Free)

In our lives we are overwhelmed by social media updates, that we "have to check". By putting social media in a alternative audio channel, it frees up our visual bandwidth for more important things.

Features

Auto-imports your newsfeed and facebook wall

Updates your newsfeed every minute and read out loud the items

Create new columns with any group of your friends

Also works as a facebook client, update statuses and likes.
