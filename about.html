Viola started out as a weekend hack combining text to voice and facebook. Now it's way more awesome.

Get from Chrome Store (Free)

We consume media in a lot of different ways. For news, we consume them as TV, online video, articles, and newspapers. For novels, we consume them as books, ebooks, and audio.

Social media is now a heavy part of our media consumption, yet we only consume it a single way: as a visual representation on a website. Is there a need for something that translate social media visual content to audio content?

Viola is the answer to that question.
